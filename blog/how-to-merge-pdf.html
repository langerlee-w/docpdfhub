How to Merge PDF Files (Free & Private)

Combine multiple PDFs in your browser — no upload, no watermark, instant download.

Need to merge multiple PDFs into one document? With DocPDFHub’s Merge PDF tool, you can do it fully in your browser — no upload, no wait, no privacy risk.

Step 1 — Add Your Files

Drop your PDFs directly into the page or click the Select Files button. You can add multiple files at once. Reorder them by dragging if you need to change the page sequence.

Step 2 — Arrange and Merge

Once files are added, preview their order. Click Merge PDFs to combine. Processing happens entirely in your browser, so your documents never leave your device.

Step 3 — Download Result

When merging is done, your new combined PDF automatically downloads. Nothing is uploaded or stored on any server.

Tips

  • Use descriptive filenames before merging (helps keep them in the right order).
  • All files stay local — for extra-sensitive content, you can even go offline after loading the page.
  • Supports both portrait and landscape pages.
