About DocPDFHub

Free, fast, and privacy-first PDF tools that run entirely in your browser — no uploads, no servers.

What is DocPDFHub?

DocPDFHub is a collection of lightweight PDF utilities — merge, split, compress, convert to images, and more. All processing happens locally on your device using modern Web APIs (like Canvas and WebAssembly). Your files never leave your browser.

Why privacy-first?

Many online PDF tools require uploading your documents to a remote server. We believe most tasks don’t need that. Keeping everything local means improved privacy, faster results, and no waiting for uploads/downloads.

Key principles

  • 100% local: your PDFs are processed in-browser.
  • Simple: clean UI with minimal steps.
  • Fast: no network roundtrips for heavy processing.
  • Free: supported by privacy-respecting ads.
